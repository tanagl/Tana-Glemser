Virtual Character Kit

Development and evaluation of virtual character starter kit for Unity projects. Showcasing the models in high-quality videos.

Character Creator 4 Cinemachine Unity HDRP Blender/Maya

The Challenge

Creation and evaluation of different character models for use in Unity. Different virtual model software was compared in realism, suitability for use cases and integration in Unity. Final solutions developed in CC4 [1]. Development of scene and filming of video to showcase the integration of high-quality character models in different scenes, as part of an internship.

The Solution

Models created with the Character Creator 4 [1] . Video recording with Cinemachine. High-quality lighting and use of HDRP
