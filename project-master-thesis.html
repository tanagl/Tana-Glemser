Negotiation Training in VR

A contract negotiation training in fully immersive VR with an AI powered virtual human as automated negotiation partner. Developed and evaluated as part of my master's thesis.

Unity OpenAI API Meta Quest Pro Microsoft Azure

The Challenge

Real live negotiation training sessions are resource-intensive and require the presence of skilled trainers. The challenge was to create an automated system that allows users to practice negotiation skills in a virtual environment, providing realistic interactions without the need for a human partner.

The Solution

From an existing negotiation training concept (Multiuser Negotiation Training), I developed a fully immersive VR application using Unity and Meta Quest Pro. The negotiation partner was connected to the GPT-4 model from OpenAI to generate responses for context-aware conversations, through the OkGoDoIt library [2]. The system incorporates speech recognition and text-to-speech technologies from Microsoft Azure to facilitate natural interactions, allowing users to engage in realistic negotiation scenarios anytime, anywhere. Character models were created with Autodesk Character Generator. Animations are from Mixamo and lip-sync is realized with the SALSA LipSync Suite from CrazyMinnowStudio. Developed in Unity for the Meta Quest Pro.

The Evaluation

Evaluation and master thesis study (“Bot or Not: Exploring the Perceived Agency of an Embodied Artificial Intelligence in a Virtual Reality Negotiation Training”). The study explores the agency effect through framing of the negotiation agent as human-controlled or AI-controlled.
